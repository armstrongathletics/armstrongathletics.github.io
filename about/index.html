About

Armstrong Athletics

Hi, my name is Louis Armstrong, the creator of Armstrong Athletics. I am a twenty eight year old certified personal trainer from Pacifica, California. I train locally along the peninsula of California.

I have been working in the fitness industry for four years now and I love it. It is extremely rewarding to help a client achieve their fitness goals. Sports and fitness has been a part of my life since I was about nine years old, with my first experience being, when my parents enrolled me in martial arts lessons. From there I was engaged in sports all the way through high school to junior college, playing football and running track. Growing up playing football presented me with many tools that helped me when I was younger and still help me to this day.

It takes hard work to prepare for a football season, lots of hours must be dedicated to working out and practicing, many sacrifices must be made in order to be physically and mentally prepared. It takes discipline to train your mind and body to eat the proper foods, rest when its time to rest, and push your body to its limits. I applied these aspects to life, whether it was track, football, school, or relationships and work. I still compete in athletic events and sports, however I turned my focus towards personal training and helping others. I have decided to make it my goal to help as many individuals as possible achieve their fitness goals. It is my mission to provide a unique training experience for each person I train. Training is my passion and helping others is a gift. I would like to share both with you.
