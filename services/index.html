Services

Not into the gym life? Love being outdoors in the fresh air? Well that’s how I train around here. I provide outdoor training for all clients. All clients will start with a fitness evaluation, followed by a fitness test, correction of form and proper technique. This is to ensure that any advanced exercises performed later on will be safe and more effective.

You will learn fun and challenging exercises using; battle ropes, weights, bands, agility ladders, complex drills, resistance running, plyometrics, circuit training and more. Whatever you are training for, I will provide a unique program that is tailored to meet your needs. So what are you waiting for? Let’s get started, just click on the “Contact” link, and send me a message to get started.

Offered programs: fitness training, weight training, sports training, nutrition, small group training.
